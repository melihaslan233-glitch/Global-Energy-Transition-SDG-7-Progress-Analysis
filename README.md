# Global-Energy-Transition-SDG-7-Progress-Analysis
Problem Statement: Despite global efforts, the transition to sustainable energy remains inconsistent. While some nations rapidly adopt renewables, others remain heavily dependent on fossil fuels due to economic constraints, leading to a widening "green gap." This project aims to use Machine Learning models to identify the key socio-economic drivers of this disparity and forecast future $CO_2$ emission trajectories.SDG Alignment: This project is primarily aligned with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy), focusing on increasing the share of renewable energy and improving energy efficiency. It also strongly supports SDG 13 (Climate Action) by predicting carbon emissions based on energy policies.Impact Potential: The analysis provides a data-driven roadmap for identifying "high-potential" regions where targeted investments and AI-driven energy management could significantly accelerate the transition and reduce carbon footprints.
